A Safety-First Operating System

EVE-OS is the core platform developed by Hermetic Labs. It is a local-first, safety-oriented operating system designed for high-stakes and regulated environments where reliability, transparency, and human oversight are essential.

EVE-OS was created to support real-world decision-making — not abstract experimentation. It is built to operate under constraint, maintain accountability, and remain dependable even when infrastructure, connectivity, or conditions are imperfect.

Governance by Design
